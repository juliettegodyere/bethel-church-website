About Us

Bethel Bible Church is known for empowering people - with their spirits constantly fed with the right information and insight geared towards becoming role models fulfilling their God given purpose. The church started as a vision in November 1985 from a revelation by the Holy Spirit and was founded by Apostle J.C. Uka. It took a period of fasting, prayer and training before the ministry started. We started at Lawanson Road, Itire as Ambassadors For Christ in 1987 before it was changed to Bethel Bible Church in July 1988. Bethel Bible Church started in a bungalow building located at 16 Adenowo Street, Ikate, Surulere, Lagos-Nigeria - which is the present Headquarters. It has since grown in capacity and number with a branch in Oguta, Imo-Nigeria. The church was commissioned to take the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all parts of this nation, Africa and the entire world. This brought about the mission statement: sitting on the Word of God to deliver nations through raising kingdom builders.

Apostle J.C. Uka

Apostle J.C. Uka – John Chinedu Uka is the President and Vision Coordinator of Bethel Global Outreach and Bethel Bible Church with the Head Quarters in Lagos, Nigeria, West Africa. He is called to liberate and deliver mankind from the afflictions and bondages of the devil through the power in the word of God. He is a revivalist, a prolific writer with a deep prophetic and apostolic insight in the word of God that has transformed many nations in Africa and beyond. He has a Doctorate degree in Theology and Christian Education. He is married to Pastor (Mrs) Delphine Uka.

Pastor Samuel Affiah

Pastor Samuel Affiah – Samuel Affiah is the Resident Pastor of Bethel Bible Church (Headquarters) an Agriculturalist with specialization in Agronomy by training. He has been serving as an ordained pastor for about 23 years. He gave his life to Christ on Campus and was brought up by the Student Christian Movement (SCM). He has a mandate to teach the Word with integrity, manifesting Kingdom power and lifestyle. He is an alumnus of Pastors’/Ministers Training Institute-Africa (PMTI-A). He is married to Lawrencia Nene and they are blessed with three children.
